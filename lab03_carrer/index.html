About me

Hello world, my name is Domenic Cassisi. I am a visiting student from Germany and only will be here for one trimester. During my stay, I will visit three different modules. You can get more information on my courses here.

I began studying computer science at Hochschule Furtwangen University (short: HFU) in 2017. I am now in my sixth semester. In semester three, I decided to set to focus on Software Engineering which interested me really the most.
